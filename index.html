About

Care Club is an organisation founded by two keen teenagers, Zoya Patel and Prisha Somani who are working towards a better tomorrow for the underprivileged where the government fails them. We plan on giving them once in a lifetime experiences and aiding to their most diminutive of needs. We want to show them that they are erudite and capable and should never give up for we don't know what tomorrow holds for us.
